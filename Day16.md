## 참고사항
- **v** = verb (동사)
- **n** = noun (명사)
- **adj** = adjective (형용사)
- **adv** = adverb (부사)
- **prep** = preposition (전치사)
- **phr** = phrase (어구)
- **conj** = conjunction (접속사)
